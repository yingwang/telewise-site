Telewise Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Overview

Telewise is a Telegram client for Wear OS. It connects directly to Telegram's servers using the official TDLib library. We do not operate any intermediary servers.

Data Collection

Telewise does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to third parties. All communication occurs directly between your device and Telegram's servers.

Permissions

  • Internet — Required to connect to Telegram servers.
  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO) — Used for voice input when composing messages. Audio is processed on-device by the system speech recognizer and is not recorded or stored by Telewise.
  • Vibrate — Used for haptic feedback when sending messages.

Data Storage

Telegram session data and message cache are stored locally on your watch using TDLib's built-in database. This data remains on your device and is removed when you uninstall the app or clear app data.

Third-Party Services

Telewise communicates exclusively with Telegram's servers (telegram.org). We do not integrate any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
